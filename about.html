About us

Serving hundreds of millions of people every month, we help people buy and sell cars, find housing, get jobs, buy and sell household goods, and much more.

Did you know our company began life back in 2006? As we’ve grown, we’ve changed from a small collection of classifieds brands, to a global player with one strategy and vision.

We don’t just help our users buy and sell things, find a job, or find a service provider – we now offer many of these ourselves, and more. Our aim across all of our brands is to make secondhand trade easy, convenient, and stress-free.
